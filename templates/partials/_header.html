WHO WE ARE!

Welcome to the Annual Programming Competition, proudly organized by the Faculty of Computing and Informatics (FCI) at NUST!
This exhilarating event is designed to ignite your passion for coding and foster a spirit of innovation among aspiring programmers.
In this thrilling competition, learners and students from various high schools and tertiary institutions come together to showcase their skills in critical thinking and problem-solving.
Whether you’re a seasoned coder or just starting your programming journey, this is your chance to challenge yourself, collaborate with peers, and push the boundaries of what’s possible.
